### The "Sandbag Economy" is Over
For commercial properties in Fort Myers, the math has changed. A 3-foot [commercial flood gate system](/commercial-flood-gates) acts as a specialized bulkhead. Unlike plywood or sandbags, these engineered systems:
1.  **Deploy in Minutes:** Crucial for businesses that need to stay open until the last safe moment.
2.  **Reduce Premiums:** Many insurers offer mitigation credits for approved dry-floodproofing.
3.  **Prevent Mold:** Sealing the water *out* prevents the tens of thousands of dollars in mold remediation often required even after "minor" seepage.

With the Caloosahatchee River levels fluctuating more unpredictably, experts advise businesses to secure their perimeter now, rather than competing for supplies in June.
